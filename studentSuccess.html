In early August, I chose to complete the entirety of my junior year as a virtual student. So, here are the tips that I have taken up to ensure my junior year is a success.

Routine: This is by far the most important tip. I wake up at a consistent time as if I was going to physical school. I also make sure to change out of my pajamas and into something that I feel prepares me for the day.
Time Blocking: With being a virtual student comes a lot of freedom. I try to plan how I will tackle assignments by assigning chunks of time to complete them.
